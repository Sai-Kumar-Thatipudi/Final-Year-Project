About Our System

The Smart Agriculture Prediction System is an AI-powered solution designed to assist farmers and investors in making informed decisions about crop selection and yield prediction. By leveraging machine learning, this system provides accurate insights based on soil quality, climate conditions, and historical agricultural data.
Features
Crop Recommendation for Farmers
Selecting the right crop is essential for maximizing productivity. This system analyzes key factors such as soil nutrients, temperature, humidity, rainfall, and pH levels to recommend the most suitable crop. Using a K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithm, the system ensures that farmers receive scientifically backed recommendations tailored to their specific land conditions.
